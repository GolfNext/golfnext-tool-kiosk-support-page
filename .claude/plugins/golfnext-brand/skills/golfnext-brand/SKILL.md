# GolfNext Brand Skill

This skill contains the complete GolfNext brand identity. It ensures every piece
of content is on-brand in both design and communication.

## How to Use This Skill

When creating or reviewing content, load the relevant reference file(s):

| Task | Reference to Read |
|------|-------------------|
| Writing any text (emails, posts, docs, presentations) | `references/tone-of-voice.md` |
| Designing visuals, choosing colors, using logos | `references/design-guidelines.md` |
| Describing who GolfNext is, our mission, story | `references/core-narrative.md` |
| Creating a full deliverable (e.g. pitch deck) | Read ALL three reference files |

## Quick Reference Card

Keep these values handy for any task:

### Brand Essence
- **Payoff:** "The Future In Play"
- **Trust line:** "By Golfers - For Golfers"
- **Core positioning:** Open platform vs. closed ecosystems
- **Three-word voice:** Credible. Knowledgeable. Human.

### Writing Style at a Glance
- Lead with value, not features ("Your players can pay faster" not "We updated the API")
- Short, punchy sentences. Every word earns its place.
- Use "you" and "your club" - never formal address
- Warm but confident. Like a knowledgeable colleague, not a vendor.
- Real stories and numbers over abstract claims
- When things go wrong: acknowledge, fix, follow up. No lengthy apologies.

## Important Rules

1. **Tone of Voice takes precedence** over the old brand guide's tone section.
   The authoritative source for voice and writing style is `references/tone-of-voice.md`.

2. **Core Narrative is the final draft** (the "Open Future" version).
   When describing GolfNext's story, mission, or positioning, use `references/core-narrative.md`.

3. **Design guidelines** from the brand guide remain fully valid for all visual decisions.
   Colors, typography, logo usage, photography, and graphical elements: `references/design-guidelines.md`.

4. **Never use competitor trash-talk.** Acknowledge competitors with class, focus on GolfNext strengths (openness, freedom of choice, golf expertise).

5. **"By Golfers - For Golfers"** is the emotional anchor. Always maintain the human, golf-passionate side alongside the tech/innovation narrative.
