# Core Narrative: Driving the Open Future of Golf

> This is the authoritative version of the GolfNext core narrative.
> Use this when describing who GolfNext is, our mission, story, and positioning.

## The Vision: What's Next for the Game

Golf is built on tradition, but progress defines its future.

At GolfNext, we believe the golf industry deserves open, flexible technology that supports how clubs and players actually want to operate -- today and tomorrow.

Our mission is to connect the golf ecosystem through technology, accessibility, and freedom of choice.

Not by locking clubs into rigid systems -- but by giving them the ability to evolve.

While others build closed ecosystems, we build an open platform designed for what comes next.

---

## The Problem: When Technology Limits Progress

Too many golf clubs are locked into all-or-nothing technology decisions.

Choose one vendor's hardware, and you're locked into their software.
Want to change? Replace everything.

This limits innovation, increases costs, and removes leverage.

GolfNext takes a different approach.

Our hardware and infrastructure stay with you -- while you remain free to choose the software that best fits your club.

That freedom creates real value:
- Better negotiating power
- Faster adoption of new technology
- Lower long-term costs

**Your infrastructure. Your choice. Your future.**

---

## The Solution: An Open Platform Built to Evolve

GolfNext is built on three core commitments:

**Freedom to Choose**
Switch software without replacing hardware. We integrate new solutions into the GolfNext platform -- not the other way around.

**Power to Connect**
We integrate with any partner who wants to integrate with us. And when an integration doesn't exist yet, we build it.

**Ready for What's Next**
New needs, new technologies, new business models -- GolfNext ensures clubs are never stuck waiting for permission to evolve.

---

## The Platform: Self-Service for Modern Golf

Modern golfers expect the same convenience they experience everywhere else.

GolfNext enables golf venues to deliver that experience -- while reducing staffing pressure and increasing revenue.

Our platform includes:

**Self-Service Kiosks**
24/7 digital reception for green fees, rentals, and pro shop purchases -- the only solution of its kind in golf.

**Driving Range Payments**
Smart payments, compatible with any ball dispenser brand, support cards, apps, wallets, PIN codes, and real-time analytics.

**Voucher Management**
Fully digital issuing, distribution, and redemption -- from single tickets to sponsor packages.

**Simulator Management**
Automated access control ensures simulators are only active during paid sessions -- eliminating time theft and maximizing utilization.

---

## Proof: Delivering Value at Scale

Founded in Denmark in 2012 by Peter Semler, GolfNext began with a simple idea: self-service check-in and payment for golfers.

Today, it is the industry's most experienced self-service platform:

- 400+ venues across 18 countries
- 350,000+ active users (2025)
- 500+ million golf balls dispensed annually through our range solutions

As golf continues to modernize, demand is accelerating -- both for self-service solutions and for replacing outdated, cash-based driving range technology. We're helping clubs modernize operations, one venue at a time.

## Why GolfNext -- Why Now

GolfNext reflects who we are today and where we're going.

As our platform and ambitions expanded internationally, clarity became essential. The similarity between "GolfMore" and "Golfamore" created market confusion -- and through a positive, mutual agreement, we used that moment to evolve.

But GolfNext is more than a new name.

It signals our role as a long-term digital partner for clubs, ranges, and players -- and our commitment to building an open platform that evolves as new needs and technologies emerge.

To support this next phase, we've strengthened our foundation.

VaekstPartner Kapital has joined as a strategic co-owner, enabling faster international growth. Peter Semler continues as Founder and Chief Product Officer, ensuring openness and product innovation remain central, while Carsten Boe Jensen leads the company as CEO, focusing on scale, partnerships, and operational excellence.
